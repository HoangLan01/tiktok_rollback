# Functional Specifications

## 1. Hero Summary (Home)
- Large Central Metric: Total Views (Triệu lượt xem) with gradient text.
- Sub-metrics: Likes, Shares, Comments, Saves, Total Videos (Grid layout).

## 2. Brand Analysis Dashboard
- Feature: Horizontal Bar Chart showing video count per car brand.
- Metrics: Display "Average Views" (avg) per brand on the right side of each bar.
- AI Logic: Flag brands with high engagement (e.g., "Lynk & Co" with 410K avg) using a fire icon.

## 3. Top 5 Hall of Fame
- List ranking from 01 to 05.
- Elements: Title, View Count (M), Like Count (K), Date (DD/MM/YYYY).
- Content Tags: Dynamic badges (Storytelling, Lifestyle, Drama, Sponsored, Counter-argument) with distinct colors.
